## PROVEN invariants (Qed) -- any '!!' line below would be a RocqRefRunner bug:
##   HALFOPEN      => FILTER       (b64_passes_through_hot_pixel_halfopen_compute_implies_closed)
##   HALFOPEN_EXACT => EXACT       (b64_liang_barsky_touches_halfopen_implies_closed)
##
## Empirical rounded-vs-exact divergences (the hardening signal, NOT bugs):
##   FILTER_OVERACCEPT  : closed filter TRUE, exact FALSE -- rounded b64_div closes a
##       sub-ulp tangency gap inward.  Conservative/SAFE for a noder; machine-checked
